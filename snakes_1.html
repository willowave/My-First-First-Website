"Although there are those amongst us who would extol the virtues of these creatures, to a far greater amount of people, snakes quite simply represent the stuff of nightmares. Whether it is the way that they move or those stark, unblinking eyes, they just seem to represent the absolute definition of everything that is dangerous and should be avoided at all costs."

We are the -SnakeS- of uTanks, originally a team from Battlefield led by BBC3, -AnacondA-, and -VipeR-. The team's creation stemmed from the break-up of the Dynasty team in 2001 during the days of Battlefield.

During this time the -SnakeS- lasted through years of Battlefield enduring leadership changes due to BBC3 and -AnacondA-'s retirement. -VipeR- took the team over in 2003 and did a phenomenal job leading the team into battle against the most known team at the time, STRIKES led by falon.

As the days of Battlefield came to an end so did the -SnakeS-. Recently, the -SnakeS- were brought back at the end of August 2011 by the original -AnacondA- and -VipeR- on uTanks. Due to -AnacondA-'s detrimental choices the team nearly fell apart; as a result, -AnacondA- retired his tank as it was permanently deleted and banned.

-VipeR- and -Death addeR- rose to the occasion to keep the team together, and have turned the team around in a new, great positive direction. We the -SnakeS- have many plans for the future and hope to build a legacy. See you all on the field!

Our team goal is to conquer Top 25 Blitzkrieg and Top 25 World. We are hoping to remain one of the game's most active teams. Leading teams in Top 25 of Blitzkrieg, sword count, and cup count we hope to continue leading teams in these categories.

We also hope to keep this game active as we have done so far. As of right now, we are very satisfied with the progress this team has made. The leaders of the -SnakeS- have much bigger plans once final release is out!
