The legend of The Gods began many years ago…. They came from the world before μTanks… the old world, one of renown men, the world of legends and warriors… the world of “Battlefield”. They are the legends that fought colossal raids and lived to tell about it. As the most feared and respected fighters from the old world they were given a gift… the gift of immortality, making their reign everlasting! 

As the most revered fighters of the old world, they have now become the most feared fighting force in the new world, these legendary warriors have become reincarnated in the world of μTanks as The Gods! Now on the verge of chaos, The Gods have furthered their reign of destruction by taking over the battlefield. Ruling it with an iron fist, striking down their foes and crushing all who oppose them!

They only show favor to those who are worthy, those who play as they do, only to warriors who fight and die with honor, regardless of the odds… The Gods are the elite. Get in their way and you will surely see your own mortality in death. If you gain their respect, they may… just may impart you with immortality to live among the legends, among the ETERNAL.

They are the ELITE, they are The Gods. You can run, but they will catch you. You can hide, but they will find you. Say your prayers.
